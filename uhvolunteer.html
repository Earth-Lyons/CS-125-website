I volunteer as a Care Rounder

In this position, I refer to myself as part of the care support team because this position greatly revolves around being attentive to the needs of the patients. I am responsible for providing non-clinical support for the patients, hoping to provide comfort during their time of need. Bringing companionship during an emotional period for the patients improves my ability to be compassionate and offer some reassurance. It is a wonderful experience to be able to listen to the patients, their stories, and sometimes having a laugh to encourage a friendly atmosphere. Definitely, being strong in communication is the most important aspect of the experience, and I strongly exhibit such a skill to not only interact with the patients to hopefully offer any assistance but also to keep in contact with the RNs and CNAs to relay pertinent information to provide a better patient experience.
